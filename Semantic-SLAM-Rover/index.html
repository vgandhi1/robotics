Semantic SLAM Rover

A rover that doesn't just map space — it understands what's in it. YOLOv8 at 45 FPS on a Jetson Orin Nano fuses detections with LiDAR to place class-labeled 3D landmarks into a live map.

01
The Gap

Geometry isn't understanding

A standard warehouse robot sees LiDAR geometry — walls, columns, obstacles. It cannot tell a concrete pillar from a human. This project fuses vision semantics with LiDAR so Nav2 can act on commands like "find the fire extinguisher and stop 0.5 m in front of it."
